# Projections

## Overview

Projections help you forecast your financial future based on recurring transactions and historical data.

## Features

### Income Projection

See your expected income for: - Current month - Next 3 months - Next 6 months - Next 12 months

### Expense Projection

Track expected expenses across: - By category - Total monthly expenses - Daily burn rate

### Balance Projection

Forecast your account balance based on: - Current balance - Projected income - Projected expenses

### Projection Periods

  • Monthly View: Current month breakdown

  • Quarterly View: 3-month outlook

  • Yearly View: 12-month forecast

## How Projections Work

  1. System identifies all active recurring transactions

  2. Projects future dates based on recurrence rules

  3. Aggregates by category and time period

  4. Displays projected totals and balances

### Projection Calculation

` Projected Income = Sum of all recurring income Projected Expense = Sum of all recurring expenses Projected Balance = Current Balance + Projected Income - Projected Expense `

## Visualization

### Charts

  • Line charts for balance trends

  • Bar charts for income vs expenses

  • Pie charts for category breakdown

### Summary Cards

  • Total projected income

  • Total projected expenses

  • Net projection
